Boosts are strong bonuses that only last a limited amount of rooms.

You cannot have two instances of the same Boost active at the same time. If you pick up a Boost that you already have active it increases the duration of your current Boost by that many rooms.

The Ooze Power Special Spice increases the effectiveness of Boosts, and the Ooze Power Shelf Life and Insulated Pizza Box Artifact can increase their duration.
