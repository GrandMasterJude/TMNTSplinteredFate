TMNT: Splintered Fate – Inspirations

Each character starts with their own two Inspirations. At three points during a run an additional Inspiration from another turtle will be offered as a reward.

These are offered from the Gauntlet Mini-boss in The Sewers, the first room of The Docks, and both the regular and Gauntlet Mini-boss of The Streets or The Junkyard.

Level 1

Cost: 200 need: 3

Cost: 450 need: 2

Cost: 700 need: 4

+15% Attack area size. +15% Attack damage.
