Each Turtle starts with their own two Inspirations. At three points during a run an additional Inspiration from another turtle will be offered as a reward.

This are offered from the Gauntlet Mini-boss in The Sewers, the first room of The Docks, and both the regular and Gauntlet Mini-boss of The Streets.

Michelangelo Inspiration 1

Level 1

Cost: 250 need: 3

+5% Attack area size. +5% Attack damage.

Level 2

Cost: 600 need: 2

+10% Attack area size. +10% Attack damage.

Level 3

Cost: 1000 need: 4

+15% Attack area size. +15% Attack damage.
