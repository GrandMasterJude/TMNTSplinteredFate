How the math in the game works isn't necessaryily intuitive. If you find yourself wondering why your damage numbers aren't adding up the way you think, this might help explain it.

Damage Formulas

Attack: Base Damage + Upgrade Bonus + Crit Bonus = Total damage

So for example, Raphael's basic attack deals 24 damge. When he does a Critical Hit it will do 49 damage

24 base damage + 25 crit damage (24 base * 1.06) *Raph gets the extra 6% from his inspiration.

After he buys some upgrades and has +50% attack though, his first attack will do 36 damage but his crits will only do 61.

Your total damage with the attack upgrade doesn't get multiplied by 1.06 like you might assume.

24 base damage + 12 upgrade damage + 25 crit damage (24 base * 1.06 crit)
